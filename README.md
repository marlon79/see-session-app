# see-session-app
SeeSession is the ultimate integration companion for Seestar smartscope users. Easily log imaging sessions, parse FITS metadata, track integration progress, and gamify your journey with achievements. Designed for clarity, data portability, and expert processing workflows. Optimize your Seestar smartscope astrophotography.


🌟 Core Features

Automated Session Logging: Forget manual entry. Drag and drop your FITS files, and our intelligent parser extracts technical metadata like altitude, filter, exposure time, and telescope model instantly.

Comprehensive Session Tracking: Log every detail of your night, including:
Targets: Easily search and link objects from our deep-space database.

Technical Specs: Track Seestar model (S50/S30), mount mode, filter usage (UV/IR Cut/LP), and bortle scale.

Exposure Metrics: Record sub-exposures, rejected lights, and calculate net integration time automatically.

Session Quality: Use our 5-star rating system to track transparency and guiding success.

Achievement System: Gamify your astrophotography journey. Unlock Bronze, Silver, Gold, and Platinum tiers across multiple categories as you build your total integration time and target library.


🛠 Advanced Tools

ImageSolver Calculator: Calculate grand integrated mean midpoints for your post-processing software (PixInsight/Siril).

Calendar View: Visualize your observation history and plan future nights.

Progress Tracking: Visualize your imaging goals for specific targets with progress bars.

Data Portability: Export your session data as structured .txt reports, styled PDFs, or Excel/Sheets-compatible files.

Responsive Dashboard: Designed for both desktop and mobile, ensuring you can log your sessions in the field or at your workstation.


💰 Subscription & Trial

SeeSession will be a premium service designed to provide a professional-grade experience for dedicated enthusiasts.

14-Day Free Trial: Start your journey with full access to all features completely free for 14 days. No data is lost when the trial ends—everything you log remains safely in your account.

Flexible Subscription Plans: We offer two recurring subscription plans (Monthly and Yearly) tailored to your imaging frequency. Upgrade within the app to unlock unlimited session logging and exclusive advanced features.

🛠 Tech Stack
SeeSession is built on the Base44 platform using React, Tailwind CSS, and a robust entity-based backend, ensuring high performance, responsive design, and secure data management.
